# Sport terminology

Use domain-specific terms when they improve clarity for target users while remaining understandable to cross-disciplinary roles.

## Choosing terms

- Prefer the term used by practitioners ("roster" for American football).
- Avoid sport slang that may not translate.
- Provide a generic label if the surface is cross-sport.
