# Glossary

Definitions for terms referenced in guidelines.

## Active voice
Subject performs the action: Subject + Verb + Object.
**Example:** The athlete submitted the RPE.

## Passive voice
Subject receives the action; often less direct.
**Example:** The RPE was submitted by the athlete.

## Plain language
Words and structure that a wide audience (approx. 7th grade reading level) can quickly understand.

## Sentence case
Capitalize only the first word and proper nouns.
**Example:** Add new injury

## Truncation
Shortening text with an ellipsis (…) when space is limited.

## Contraction
Shortened word using an apostrophe.
**Examples:** can't, it's, you're

## En dash (–)
Used for numeric ranges.
**Example:** 11:30 am–1:05 pm

## Ampersand (&)
Symbol for "and". Avoid in UI copy; spell out "and".

## Punctuation
Marks (., !, ?) used to structure meaning. Restrict punctuation in headings, button text, and labels.
