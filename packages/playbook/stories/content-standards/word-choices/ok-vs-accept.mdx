# OK vs Accept

Use "OK" when acknowledging information that does not require legal agreement. Use "Accept" when a user must agree to terms or policies.

## OK

Acknowledge informational messages.

**Do** OK (security notice)
**Don't** Accept (security notice)

## Accept

Confirm legal or policy agreement.

**Do** Accept terms
**Don't** OK terms
