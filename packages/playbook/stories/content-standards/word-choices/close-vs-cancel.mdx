# Close vs Cancel

Use "Close" when dismissing view-only content with no changes. Use "Cancel" to discard edits.

## Close

- View-only modal with read-only info
- Non-editable panel

**Do** Close (read-only modal)
**Don't** Cancel (read-only modal)

## Cancel

Discard unsaved changes.

**Do** Cancel (editable form)
**Don't** Close (editable form)
