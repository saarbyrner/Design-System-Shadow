# Spelling and formatting

Consistency in spelling and emphasis reduces cognitive friction.

## Locale

- External marketing & docs: American English (color, organize).
- Product UI baseline `en`: British English (colour, organise) unless a widely adopted technical term differs (email, API remain standard).

## Reference

Use Merriam-Webster (US) or Oxford English (UK) based on context. When in doubt, pick one and document in the Glossary.

## Emphasis

Use bold sparingly to highlight critical labels or warnings. Prefer hierarchy (headings) and layout over typographic emphasis.

**Do** Bold a single critical warning phrase.
**Don't** Bold entire paragraphs.

## Capitalization recap

Sentence case for headings, buttons, and labels. Proper nouns capitalized.
